How It Works

How are rewards calculated?

With every tap, 2% of your wager will be transferred to the reward pool. Every player increases the total pool with their taps.

How to win?

The higher was your tap payout - the higher is your potential placement. For example:

  • Bob wagers 5 $TAPS and wins 3000 $TAPS, placing rank #1.
  • Alice wagers 1 $TAPS and wins 6666 $TAPS, which makes her claim #1 away from Bob.

Reward Distribution

Rewards are distributed every day at 2AM UTC, which immediately starts a new race.
